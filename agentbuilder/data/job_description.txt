Job Description

What is the opportunity?

As a senior frontend developer within the UX/UI team under the Innovation and Technology group, you're poised to drive transformative change at the forefront of new technologies within RBC. This role offers a unique opportunity to take lead on the development of user-centric interfaces that redefine the everyday experience of RBC employees. You'll collaborate closely with designers and backend engineers, leveraging your expertise to create sleek, intuitive, and responsive interfaces that enhance user engagement and satisfaction. With a focus on innovation, you'll have the chance to pioneer new technologies and methodologies for a variety of different applications that are data-centric and client facing.

What will you do?

Take charge in the development of cutting-edge frontend interfaces within RBC's Innovation and Technology group and Wealth Management.
Collaborate closely with UX/UI designers to translate design concepts into intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces, adding animations and other micro-interactions to enhance the experience further.
Utilize your expertise in frontend technologies to create responsive and accessible web applications.
Drive innovation by utilizing and exploring new frontend frameworks, tools, and techniques.
Mentor junior developers, fostering a culture of learning and growth within the team.
Work closely with backend developers to ensure seamless integration of frontend and backend systems.
Conduct code reviews and enforce best practices to maintain high-quality code standards.
Stay abreast of industry trends and emerging technologies to continuously enhance the bank's internal digital offerings.

What do you need to succeed?

Must have

Demonstrated ability in written and oral communication skills along with strong presentation skills. Ability to determine the information and communication needs of the stakeholders and project.
3-4 years experience in HTML5/SASS/CSS and producing pixel-perfect interfaces according to design wireframes.
3-4 years experience in TypeScript and JavaScript with a strong deep understanding of the intricacies of both languages.
2-3 years experience in either React, VueJS or Angular frameworks.
3-4 years experience in using Git or other version control systems.

Nice-to-have

Bachelor degree in Software Engineering or Computer Science.
Experience in Agile methodology and the software development lifecycle.
Experience in technology and/or financial services industry.
Backend development experience in Node.JS, Python 3 or C#.
Strategic thinker with excellent interpersonal skills to work across functions and businesses.
